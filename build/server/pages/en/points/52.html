Antenore's victory over Veleso

 
The work portrays the last episode of Antenore's journey and sees the two protagonists on horseback at the right end of the composition. The hero leans with his steed on the defeated challenger, who is about to fall from his horse. On the right there is a herald, in colorful clothing, intent on playing a trumpet towards the background while the continuation of the battle is represented in the background. As in the first two frescoes, there is a reference to 1650, a date which is found imprinted on a banner. The painting differs from the previous ones for the movement and dynamism, obtained by means of the positioning of the horses, by their continuous crossing and by the use of very clear, bright colors and in some points perfectly contrasted.
